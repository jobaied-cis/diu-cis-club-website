Our Mission

The CIS Club aims to build a strong tech-driven community by hosting workshops, seminars, coding contests, cultural programs, and sports activities that help students grow personally and academically.

Our Vision

To create future leaders in technology who are skilled, confident, and ready to contribute to the digital world.

Our Achievements

  • Organized 50+ successful events
  • Trained 500+ students in programming & AI
  • Hosted inter-university tech fest
  • Won DIU Club of the Year Award — 2024

History of the CIS Club

The CIS Club was founded to provide a dedicated platform for students to explore and develop their technical skills. Over the years, we have organized numerous tech workshops, programming contests, industry tours, and peer-learning sessions. Today, the CIS Club stands as one of the most dynamic communities in our department.
